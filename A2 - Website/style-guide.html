Brand

Offering

We provide an open community lab for interdisciplinary and exploration collaboration to share ideas, knowledge, and equipment. We offer the space, workshops, and equipment you need to make your own discoveries!

Our Members

The company is a community-based non-profit that values dicovery and diversity. They invite people from various displines to engage in their workshops and are also an educational outreach group for schools.

Why us?

The company is clear about their community and values in content to bring in people wih shared beliefs. This invloves showcasing projects from the community so that people know what they could do as a member. Their are also different types of offerings in workshops and membership that appeal to different audiences.
